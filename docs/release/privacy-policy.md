## 1. Who this applies to
This policy covers the **DotMac Self-Care** mobile app (`io.dotmac.selfcare`) and the
self-care web portal used by DotMac internet customers and resellers to manage their
service, billing, and support.

## 2. Information we collect
We collect only what is needed to provide the service:

| Category | Examples | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Account & contact** | Name, email, phone, date of birth, gender (optional), service/billing address | Identify your account, contact you, bill the correct address |
| **Service location** | A map pin / GPS coordinates **you choose to share** (foreground only) | Locate your service address for installs and fault dispatch |
| **Billing & payments** | Invoices, payments, wallet balance, saved-card *references* (tokens), bank-transfer receipts you upload | Process payments and keep your billing record |
| **Usage data** | Your internet data consumption, session/usage summaries | Show your data usage and plan status |
| **Support content** | Tickets, messages, and files/photos you attach | Provide customer support |
| **Device & app** | Push notification token, app/diagnostic logs, device model/OS | Send notifications, diagnose crashes |
| **Photos/camera** | Profile photo and support attachments you select or capture | Only when you choose to add them |

**We do NOT collect or store full card numbers.** Card payments are processed by our
payment providers (see §4); the app only receives a tokenized reference.

We use **foreground location only** — never in the background — and only after you grant
permission and choose to attach a location.

## 3. How we use your information
- Provide and maintain your internet service and account.
- Process payments, top-ups, and reseller settlements.
- Send service, billing, and support notifications.
- Verify service-location pins and dispatch technicians.
- Diagnose and fix crashes and errors.
- Comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations (e.g. withholding-tax records).

We do **not** sell your personal data, and we do **not** use it for third-party advertising.

## 4. Third parties we share data with (processors)
We share the minimum necessary with service providers who process data on our behalf:

| Provider | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Paystack**, **Flutterwave** | Payment processing | Payment amount, email, payment method |
| **Google Firebase Cloud Messaging** | Push notifications | Device push token |
| **GlitchTip / Sentry (self-hosted)** | Crash & error diagnostics | Diagnostic logs, device/app info |
| **Geocoding provider** (OpenStreetMap Nominatim / Google, per configuration) | Convert addresses ↔ coordinates | Address text or coordinates you submit |

Each provider has its own privacy policy. We may also disclose data where required by law.

## 5. Data retention
We keep account, billing, and tax records for as long as your account is active and as
required by Nigerian law and tax/accounting obligations after closure. Support and
diagnostic data are retained only as long as needed for those purposes.

## 6. Security
Access is authenticated and role-scoped; sensitive credentials are encrypted at rest;
payment card data is tokenized by our payment providers and not stored by us.
