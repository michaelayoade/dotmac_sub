How It Works

If a bank row says INV-10045, the system first checks invoice number if that is your first rule.

If there is no invoice number, it can try account number, then phone, then email in the order you choose.

If nothing matches clearly, the payment stays for manual review instead of being attached to the wrong customer.

Simple Example

Bank narration: Transfer 08031234567 March payment

Rule order: Invoice Number, Account Number, Phone, Email

Result: the system skips invoice and account number, finds the phone number, and suggests that customer.
