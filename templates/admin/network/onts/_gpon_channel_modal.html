SmartOLT allocates ONU IDs from the range 0-127 for the GPON channel and from the range 128-255 for the XG/XGS-PON channels.

Once the 128-255 range is exhausted, it allocates from the 0-127 range for both GPON and XG/XGS-PON channels.

Hint: XGPON-capable ONUs that were previously authorized on the GPON channel should be changed to the XGPON channel to free up IDs for the GPON ONUs.
