> puts each field engineer's day in one place and keeps dispatch in sync.
>
> • Today view — your scheduled jobs, ordered by route, with customer and site detail
> • Turn-by-turn navigation to each work order
> • On-site evidence — capture photos and completion proof against the job
> • Materials — log what you consumed on site
> • Worklogs & transitions — start, pause, and complete jobs with accurate timestamps
> • Live location while on shift so dispatch can coordinate and verify work
> • Works offline — actions queue and sync when you're back on signal
> • Push notifications for new dispatches and schedule changes
>
